Light Conical Tensile Structures (Pagoda)

Light Conical Tensile Structures:

A Light Conical Tensile Structures (Pagoda) , or pagoda , is a lightweight version of a traditional conical structure, often used for temporary or decorative purposes at events or in gardens. The fabric is stretched over a central pole to create a sharp, clean roof profile.

The Light Conical Tensile Structure (Pagoda) features a sharp, conical shape that is both elegant and functional. It provides visual appeal with its unique roof design while offering stability and ease of installation.

They are lightweight, cost-effective, and provide great aesthetic value. Their simple design offers easy installation while still providing reliable protection against weather elements like rain and sun.

These structures are widely used in events, outdoor festivals, pop-up shops, weddings, trade shows, and temporary exhibition booths etc. due to their easy setup and attractive design.
