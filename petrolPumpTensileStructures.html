A Petrol Pump Tensile Structure is a highly durable canopy system that provides weather protection for fuel dispensing areas. It creates a modern, branded, and aesthetically appealing appearance for petrol stations.

These structures are made from waterproof, UV-resistant PVC fabric that offers protection from sun, rain, and wind. This ensures customers and fuel dispensing equipment are shielded from weather elements.

Yes, we can customize the structure with brand colors, logos, and advertising graphics. This creates a strong brand identity for petrol stations and improves visibility.

These structures are used in petrol pumps, CNG stations, fuel filling stations, and gas stations etc. to provide a safe, shaded, and comfortable environment for customers and employees.

Yes, these structures are made with epoxy-painted frames and high-tensile fabric that offer long-term durability. They withstand extreme weather and provide lasting protection for petrol stations.
